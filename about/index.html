About Us | Liquacious

Liquacious is a dynamic newspaper organization dedicated to transforming community insights and ideas into professional news narratives. Composed of a diverse team of talented members including Scarlett Lo, Scarlett Chen, Emma Zhao, Madison Ling, Pheeny Yuen, Chloe Chen, Alex Zhang, and Johann Lo, Liquacious emphasizes collaboration and inclusivity in journalism. The organization prides itself on engaging with its audience, actively seeking feedback and suggestions to enhance its reporting.

By fostering a participatory approach, Liquacious not only informs readers but also gives them a platform to share their perspectives. The brand's commitment to quality journalism ensures that current events are covered comprehensively and thoughtfully. Whether it's local happenings or broader societal issues, Liquacious aims to deliver accurate and impactful news, ensuring that every voice has the opportunity to be heard.
